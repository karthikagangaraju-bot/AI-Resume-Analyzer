# 📄 AI Resume Analyzer

**AI Resume Analyzer** is a Streamlit app that evaluates your resume against a job description. It provides:

- **Match Score** – how well your resume fits the job  
- **Matched Skills** – skills that match the job requirements  
- **Missing Skills** – skills your resume is missing  
- **Suggestions to Improve** – guidance to improve your resume  
- **Generated Resume Suggestion** – AI-generated improved resume  
- **AI Assistant** – ask questions about your resume (requires OpenAI API key)

---

## Features

1. Upload your resume (PDF, DOCX, TXT).  
2. Paste the job description.  
3. Analyze Resume: see **Match Score**, **Matched/Missing Skills**, **Suggestions**, and **Improved Resume**.  
4. Optional AI Assistant to answer your resume-related questions.
